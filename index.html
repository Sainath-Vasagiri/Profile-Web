    Title : Deep Learning for Autonomous Driving System

    Description :

    The current decade witnessed rapidly increasing in the self-driving vehicle technology mainly works under the advances of deep learning. The main objective of this paper is to survey the current

    state of the deep learning applications used in the autonomous driving system. We start by the deep learning based self-driving architectures and combinational of the neural networks, as well as

    the deep reinforcement learning method. And these methodologies forms a base for the self driving scene perception, path planning and behaviour of the motion controlled algorithms. And we

    investigate both the perception of self driving architecture and path planning that means each module will be built by using the deep learning technologies as well as end to end systems.

    This can directly map the sensory information to all of the self driving commands. Additionally we focus on the current challenges of the AI-based architectures of designing for the autonomous

    driving system. And such as their safety requirement, training data and its computational hardware. And the comparison presented in the survey helps to gain the strengths and limitations of the

    deep learning and AI approaches the designing choices for the autonomous driving system.
